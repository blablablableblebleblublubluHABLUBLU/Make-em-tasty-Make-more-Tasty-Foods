About Taco:

Tacos is a Mexican food consisting of a small hand sized corn or wheat based tortillia topped with a filling. Taco can have variety of fillings including veggies, beans, pork, beef, seafood, and cheese along with the condiments such as slsa, guacamole, or sour cream. Tacos are thought to come from Mexico, long before the spanish arrived and it orginated from Mexican silver miners in the 18th century. Before, Tacos were known as the food of the working class which includes the miners. This resulted in their portable street food being called as "tacos de minero" or "miner's tacos".

The taco was first introduced to the united states in 1905. Mexican migrants were coming into work on railroads and other jobs and started to bring their delicious food with them.


Ingredients:

>GROUND BEEF- The ground beef is the most important ingredient in this dish so you better not forget it!

>TACO SEASONING- To season the taco meat, you'll need some chili powder, cumin, garlic, powder, dried oregano, onion powder, salt and pepper.

>SAUCE FOR TACO- For the sauce, you will be needing tomato sauce and water to make your taco meat moist and flavoful.

>TORTILLAS- don't forget the tortillas! You can use crunshy or soft tortillas.

>TOPPINGS- The toppings will make your taco taste even better, you can use shredded cheese (any cheese will do), shredded letuce, chopped tomatoes, avocado, guacamole, pico e gallo or salsa, sour cream, fresh cilantro, chopped onion such as red, gree, yellow onion.


Procedure:

1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef and cook until its color turned brown.tip: don't cook the taco meat too long or it will start to dry out.

2. Reduce the heat to medium and start adding the seasonings, tomato sauce and water. After adding these, start mixing them all together and cook it for about 5 minutes more until the meat if fully cooked.

3. After that, you can now turn off the stove and start serving your taco in the tortilla, you can also put the toppings that you wanted. And there we have it! An easy homemade taco!
